About Me

Hello my name is Brandon Lor, outside of work I am someone who is curious to learn new ways to challenge myself in creativity and thinking whether that be learning a new choreography in dance or expanding my knowledge on web development. Growing up I was always a thinker, I often got lost in thought in my imagination which led me to start studying Criminal Justice at CU Denver. I was then introduced to coding through a friend. My friend introduced me to web development, and I fell in love with the creativity and challenges that coding brought forth. This led me to complete a web development bootcamp through Fullstack Academy at Colorado State University to make a career shift into web development. Using my skills below you will find projects that I've done or been apart of throughout my journey in web development.
